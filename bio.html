I'm Ryan Welte, BSU Student, Game Developer (Eventually)

Well, you clicked on this page so I'm guessing you're at least a little bit interested in my ramblings. My name is Ryan, but most call me Welte. Right now I'm going to Boise State University for a Bachelor's in Games, Interactive Media, and Mobile Technologies (GIMM). I plan on going into the video game development field once I graduate, with ambitions to eventually run a personal indie team or studio of my own.


I've always found the world of games to be something truly special, with video games in particular opening up an avenue of storytelling we could never do before. They gave us the opportunity to be a part of their grand stories in a way that we are more than just a watcher. You're a part of it, you affect it to some extent. These worlds, which at one point could have been nothing more than a set of words, can now be something tangible (to an extent, but I think you get the point).


Now with a rise in the popularity of indie devs, I've loved watching the surge of new stories, and enjoy seeing so many people create their passion projects. You can tell when a dev puts their soul into their creations, and I want to follow in those steps. I might not be a creative genius, but I love creation.


Best thing to do is to Trust the Process.
