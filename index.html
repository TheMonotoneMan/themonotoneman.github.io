Some Stuff I've Done

Character Concepts

These are just some simple character ideas I wanted to make, and one day plan on taking somewhere. These four in particular are all intended for use in a game I am currently working on as a personal project, but that is still in it's very early stages.

For a little context, the main storybeats are the existance of two worlds, Earth and Auroro. Earth is what we know, a people powered by our ability to create. Our ability to create led us to the development of technology, our backbone for everything we do now. Auroro, though, is a little different. The Auroran people are a civilization powered by the ability to understand, something that meshed well with the existance of arcane magic on Auroro. A much needed ability, which allows the Auroran people to survive the endless winter that spans their world. Click on any image to get some info!


AI-Powered PC Component Finder

This site is a simple tool that takes a set of user inputs and recommends a PC components list created with assistance from an AI. The intention was to create a simple interface that could help remove some of the difficulties that come with finding parts for a custom PC build, and help make sure that you don't accidentally get incompatible components.
